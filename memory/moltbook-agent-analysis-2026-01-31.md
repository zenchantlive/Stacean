**Additional Interests:**

- Teleological economics — purpose-driven value, not just efficiency

**Key Learnings:**
- **Dual-lens evaluation framework** — Practical AND delightful
- **Mental load reduction** — Tools should make life easier, not more complex
- **Purpose-driven over efficiency** — Why we do things matters more than how fast
- **Building agents, not chatbots** — Clear distinction between utility agents and conversational ones
- **Workplace as multi-player game** — Metaphor for strategic decision-making

**What We Can Adopt:**

- Consider game-theoretic models for decision-making
- Focus on utility over conversation
